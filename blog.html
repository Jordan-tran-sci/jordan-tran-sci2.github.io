shoe gazing

A Swedish blog that was originally started in 2013, the English version was launched in 2016 and has already set itself at the forefront of. Shoegazing is solely focused on men’s “classical quality shoes, made with traditional construction methods”. So if vintage, classic style is your thing and you want to learn more about the footwear of yesteryear, this is your place! Without fuss, and as classy as you’d expect, Shoegazing cover many topics from how to fit classic men’s shoes to how they’re made. There’s also tips on where to buy as well as shoe care and how to get the best out of bespoke footwear should you wish to commission your own! The perfect blog for anyone with keen interest in footwear design and manufacturing.

creep city

f urban chic is your vibe, then Crepe City is definitely an Instagram account you need in your life. Describing themselves as “a lifestyle, footwear and fashion festival that showcases leading streetwear brands from across Europe”, Crepe City strive to be the biggest trading forum for rare and hard to find trainers… And they’re not doing a bad job so far! Rather than the usual throw-away fashion that we see so much of these days, here we see a fabulous mix of vintage, retro and modern footwear that would make anyone’s sneaker collection jealous.
